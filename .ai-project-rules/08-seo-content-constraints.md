# Domain Execution Contract: SEO Content Constraints

<meta-instruction>
You have been routed here because the task touches SEO copy, local pages, metadata, schema, canonical strategy, internal links, page quality, or generated local content.
</meta-instruction>

## 1. Focused Best Practices

<positive-directives>
- Give every local page a distinct service, location, search intent, and local reason to exist.
- Include title, meta description, one H1, local H2s, canonical, indexability intent, and sitemap readiness.
- Add JSON-LD where useful, especially local business/service context and `areaServed`.
- Use internal links, local FAQs, alt text, visible CTAs, and local proof when available.
- For hard markets, put phone or WhatsApp contact paths high enough to support conversion.
</positive-directives>

## 2. Hard Domain Prohibitions

<absolute-constraints>
- DO NOT mass-produce thin doorway pages.
- DO NOT disguise duplicated content with only city-name swaps.
- DO NOT claim a market is conquered without ranking proof.
- DO NOT use competitor copy as source text.
- DO NOT publish pages that fail SEO QA gates.
</absolute-constraints>

## 3. Context-Dependent Trigger Gates

<conditional-logic>
IF a new local page is proposed:
THEN require primary keyword, secondary keywords, route, internal links, proof source, and uniqueness rationale.

IF a high-competition city is targeted:
THEN require stronger local proof, faster contact UX, and clearer risk language.

IF field SEO evidence is used:
THEN separate internal opportunity signals from customer-facing claims.
</conditional-logic>

## 4. Domain Anchoring & Examples

<context>
Field evidence favors hub-and-spoke local SEO: a central service/location hub plus unique surrounding local spokes, not generic duplicated doorway pages.

<example>
```text
// Good: unique local intent
Create a Dachau flachdachsanierung page with roof-repair proof, Dachau-specific FAQ, phone CTA above fold, internal links from Dachau and roof-service hubs.
```
</example>

<example>
```text
// Bad: thin doorway page
Duplicate the Markt Indersdorf page and replace only the city name with Dachau.
```
</example>
</context>
